About

A native of Malaysia, Jia Rong Gan began her violin studies at the age of five and has since captivated audiences across the United States, Europe, and Asia. After receiving her Doctor of Music from Florida State University, Jia took on the position of Artist-in-Residence with Eykamp String Quartet at University of Evansville and served as Concertmaster with the Evansville Philharmonic. Using music as a platform for cultural diversity, Jia actively participates in music ensembles that seek to promote diversity and equality on the concert stage. She also started a chamber music concert series - New Traditions, with the Eykamp String Quartet that aims to bring music by underrepresented composers to the tri-state area. Having accomplished her goals in the field of music, Jia has decided to pursue a new career in software engineering.
